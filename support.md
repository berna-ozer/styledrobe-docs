# Support

We are here to help. Most questions are answered below — if your issue is not covered, email us and we will reply within 48 hours.

## Contact

**[support@styledrobe.app](mailto:support@styledrobe.app)**

When emailing, please include:
- iPhone model and iOS version
- Styledrobe app version (Settings → bottom of screen)
- Brief description of the issue
- Screenshot if relevant

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting started

**How do I add a clothing item?**
Tap the **+** button on the Wardrobe tab and choose "Take Photo" or "Choose from Gallery." The app will automatically detect the clothing type and color.

**Why does the app ask for camera and photo permissions?**
We need camera access so you can photograph items, and photo library access so you can pick existing photos. Both are processed entirely on your device — your photos never leave your iPhone.

**Why does the app ask for location?**
Location is used only to show you the current weather, which helps the AI recommend outfits suitable for the temperature and conditions. Location is processed by Apple WeatherKit and is not stored or transmitted to us.

### AI suggestions

**The AI tagged my item incorrectly. What can I do?**
Just edit it — tap the item, change the type or color. The app learns from your corrections. After enough corrections for the same pattern, it will start suggesting your preferred label automatically.

**How does the daily outfit recommendation work?**
The app combines your wardrobe, your taste preferences (from likes and dislikes), the weather, and the occasion you select. All AI runs on your iPhone using on-device CoreML models.

**Can I get suggestions for a specific occasion?**
Yes — the Daily Outfit screen has an occasion picker (casual, business, formal, sport, date, party).

### Subscription

**What is included in the free tier?**
- 35 wardrobe items
- 3 daily outfit suggestions
- 5 saved outfits
- 3 wishlist items
- 1 saved capsule wardrobe

**What is included with Premium?**
- Unlimited items, outfits, wishlist, capsules
- Style Insights and Capsule Wardrobe Analysis
- Color Analysis (selfie-based seasonal palette)
- Trend Radar
- AI URL extraction for wishlist

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
iOS **Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Styledrobe → Cancel**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

**How do I restore a previous purchase?**
Open Styledrobe → Settings → tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID that purchased the subscription.

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes — new subscribers receive a free trial: 3 days for monthly, 7 days for yearly. One trial per Apple ID per subscription group.

**I was charged but Premium is not active.**
Wait a minute, then tap "Restore Purchases" in Settings. If still not active, email us with your transaction ID (visible in your Apple ID purchase history).

### Data and privacy

**Where is my data stored?**
On your iPhone, and (if you have iCloud Sync enabled) in your private iCloud database. Nothing is stored on our servers.

**Do you have access to my photos or wardrobe?**
**No.** All AI runs on your device. Your photos and wardrobe data are stored in your iCloud private database, which Apple encrypts end-to-end. We do not have access.

**What happens if I delete the app?**
- If iCloud Sync was off: all data is lost.
- If iCloud Sync was on: data remains in your iCloud and returns when you reinstall.

**How do I delete all my data?**
Settings → **Reset All Data**. This wipes your wardrobe, outfits, calendar, wishlist, capsules locally and propagates the deletion to your iCloud private database.

### Community feed

**What is shared when I post to the community?**
Only a composite outfit image and minimal metadata (item types, colors, occasion, weather, score). Your name, account, raw individual photos, and other wardrobe data are never shared.

**Is community sharing anonymous?**
Yes — no name, no email, no profile is attached to your post. Other users see only the outfit image and metadata.

**How do I delete a post I shared?**
Open the Community feed → tap the **...** menu on your post → **Delete post**. The post is removed from CloudKit and from other users' feeds.

**How do I report inappropriate content?**
Tap the **...** menu on any post → **Report**. Select a reason. The post is hidden from your feed immediately, and posts that receive multiple reports are automatically hidden from all users.

### Notifications

**How do I enable or disable notifications?**
Styledrobe → **Settings → Notifications**. You can toggle morning reminders, weekly unworn-clothes reminders, and rain alerts independently.

**I cancelled but I want them back, and the toggles do nothing.**
This means iOS revoked notification permission. The app shows a dialog directing you to **Settings → Styledrobe → Notifications** in iOS Settings. Enable notifications there and the toggles will work again.

### Other

**The app is in the wrong language.**
Settings → **Language**. The app supports 13 languages and follows your selection regardless of system language.

**The app crashed.**
Please email us with what you were doing when it crashed and your iPhone model. Most crashes are fixed in the next release within a week.

**I have a feature request.**
We love feedback. Email **[support@styledrobe.app](mailto:support@styledrobe.app)** and we read every message.
