# Styledrobe

AI wardrobe stylist for iPhone — runs entirely on-device.

## Why Styledrobe

- 🧠 **AI outfit suggestions** — your wardrobe items, machine-matched
- 🌅 **Daily outfit** — weather-aware, fresh every morning
- 💜 **Learns your taste** — likes/dislikes shape your scores
- 🔒 **Zero servers** — your data never reaches us
- ☁️ **iCloud-synced** — survives app reinstalls, follows your Apple ID across devices

## Privacy-first

We don't run servers. We don't see your data. We can't.

Your wardrobe lives on your iPhone, optionally syncing through your private iCloud (encrypted by Apple, inaccessible to us). No accounts, no logins, no profiles, no analytics, no ads.
